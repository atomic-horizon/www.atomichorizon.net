Combat Arena is a fast-paced, arcade-style first-person shooter where players face off in a variety of arenas set in locations from around the world. Earn coins, unlock weapons, and rise to the top of the leaderboard.

## Features

- **Diverse Weaponry** — Knock out opponents with a wide variety of weapons and unlock devastatingly powerful kill streaks.
- **Progression System** — Earn coins through matches to unlock new weapons and gear.
- **Cosmetics & Trading** — Open crates for a chance at legendary skins, then sell or trade your items with other players.
- **Cross-Platform** — Full native support for PC, console, and mobile, so you can play with friends anywhere.
- **Competitive Modes** — Multiple game modes with a Champion system that puts the match winner front and center.
